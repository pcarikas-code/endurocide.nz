Curtain Types:
1. Standard: 
   - Use: Suspended ceiling curtain track
   - Material: 100% polypropylene
   - Height: 2.0m
   - Widths: Full (7.5m), Medium (5.5m), Half (3.75m)

2. Mesh Top:
   - Use: Ceiling-fixed curtain track
   - Compliance: Mesh compliant with NFPA 13
   - Height: 2.55m (includes 0.55m mesh)
   - Widths: Full (7.5m), Medium (5.5m), Half (3.75m)

3. Long Drop:
   - Use: Ceiling-fixed curtain track
   - Material: 100% polypropylene
   - Height: 2.6m
   - Widths: Full (7.5m), Medium (5.5m), Half (3.75m)

Track Coverage:
- Full width (7.5m) covers 6.3m track
- Medium width (5.5m) covers 4.3m track
- Half width (3.75m) covers 2.3m track

Pleat Width: 15cm for all types

Colors:
- Standard: Medical Blue, Teal, Grey
- Special Order: Pastel Blue, Pastel Yellow, Latte
- Special Order Printed: Dotty on white
